Privacy Protocol

Effective Date: October 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MicroResearch Corporation. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Protocol outlines our practices regarding the data we collect when you interact with our Progressive Web Apps (PWAs), open-source contributions, and custom software solutions.

2. Data Collection

As a technology-first organization led by M Ramzan Ch, we prioritize data minimization. We collect information only necessary for:

  • Performance optimization of PWA offline-first experiences.
  • Tailoring custom software to unique organizational needs.
  • Fostering secure community collaboration in FOSS projects.
  • Analyzing Tech Innovation Pulses to improve our service delivery.

3. Progressive Web App (PWA) Security

Our PWAs utilize Service Workers and local caching. While this stores data on your device for offline use, MicroResearch Corporation does not gain unauthorized access to your local file system. All synchronized data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols before being transmitted to our secure cloud architectures.

4. Open Source & GitHub

When you interact with our repositories on GitHub, your data is subject to GitHub's privacy policy. Contributions to our FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) projects are public by nature. By contributing, you acknowledge that your GitHub profile and code commits are visible to the global developer community.

5. Data Protection Rights

Under our global standard of operation, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your data from our custom e-commerce or software platforms.
  • Object to the processing of your data for research purposes.

6. Third-Party Integrations

Our custom software solutions may include secure payment integrations or third-party APIs. These providers have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them. We only partner with world-class providers that meet our strict "Innovation Pulse" security standards.
